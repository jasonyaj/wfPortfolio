About Me:

For as long as I can remember I have always loved to break things down and see how I can rebuild them. At an early age I would break my action figures apart and mismatch them. The teenage me would spend countless all-nighters customizing and building my characters in video-games just to see how they would perform in their environment. Eventually I got into card and board games as it allowed for me scratch my itch further for building. Today I find myself revisiting coding and expanding my enjoyment for building and debugging.
